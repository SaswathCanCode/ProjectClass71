What's New?

  • Cafe Rules

    This year, in Crone Middle, we have some new rules in our Cafeteria. If you do not have money in your account, you will not be allowed to buy a snack, this rule applies everyday snack line is open, and there will be no execeptions. 2nd, you will only sit in your own seat, and may only get up to use the restroom, or leave, but only with a pass. And 3rd, lastly, you are not allowed to share food. This have been a very thing in crone due to numerous allergies, diatary restrictions, and complaints from parents.

  • Achive Period

    In Crone Middle, we have implemented a new "Achive Period" This period is after lunch for 6th Grade, before lunch for 7th Grade, and split in half before and after lunch for 8th. This period is for homeowrk, study hall, test prep, or juts a relaxing period before the rest of your classes. Although a big change, classes will continue as normal.
